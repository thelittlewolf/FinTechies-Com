About

What? Why? When? How?

What?

FinTechies.London is a resource for IT professionals within the FinTech sphere and those who want to be part of it.

Why?

The financial industry is full of ancronyms, jargon, concepts and regulations that are often alien to those not within the industry. This creates a steep learning curve for individuals joining the industry.

When?

Now(); - 24/7 through the power of the internet! And whenever we choose to meetup.

How?

FinTechies.London will produce tutorials, updates, other resources and possibly meet ups.
